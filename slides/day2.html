ATAC-seq QC - Library Complexity

  • Unique vs. Duplicate Reads
  • Library complexity can be assessed by examining the ratio of unique to duplicate reads.
  • Low complexity libraries may have many duplicate reads, suggesting PCR bias or other issues.
  • High complexity libraries will begin to plateau as sequencing depth increases.
    • This indicates that sequecnign depth is nearly saturated and additional sequencing will yield few new unique reads.

ATAC-seq QC - TSS Score

  • Transcription Start Site (TSS) Enrichment Score
  • Measures enrichment of reads at TSSs compared to flanking regions
  • ATAC-seq data is universally enriched at gene TSS regions compared to other genomic regions

Fraction of Reads in Peaks (FRiP)

  • Proportion of mapped reads that fall within called peaks
  • Higher FRiP indicates better signal-to-noise ratio
  • Performed after peak calling
